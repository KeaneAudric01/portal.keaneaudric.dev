Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2024

Important: Keane Dev has a strict no-refund policy. Please read this policy carefully before making any purchases.

1. No-Refund Policy

At Keane Dev, all payments made for our services are strictly non-refundable. By purchasing our services, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • No refunds will be issued for any reason once payment has been processed
  • This applies to all services including shared hosting, website hosting, VPS hosting, and dedicated server hosting
  • No refunds will be provided for setup fees, domain registration fees, or any add-on services
  • No refunds will be issued for service cancellations before the end of the billing period

2. Rationale for Our Policy

This strict no-refund policy is in place due to the immediate provision of services and resources upon payment. Once services are activated:

  • Server resources are immediately allocated to your account
  • Technical setup processes are initiated
  • Domain registrations or transfers are processed with third-party registrars
  • Administrative and support resources are dedicated to your account

3. Service Cancellations

You may cancel your service at any time through your client area or by contacting our support team. However:

  • Cancellation does not entitle you to a refund for any unused portion of your service
  • Your service will remain active until the end of the current billing period
  • Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 24 hours before the next billing cycle to prevent automatic renewal charges

4. Service Issues and Disputes

In cases of service issues or disputes:

  • We prioritize resolving technical issues and ensuring service performance meets our standards
  • We may offer service credits or extensions in cases of significant service disruptions at our sole discretion
  • Any compensation offered will be in the form of service extensions or account credits, not monetary refunds

5. Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

Filing a chargeback or payment dispute with your payment provider:

  • Is a violation of this policy and our Terms of Service
  • May result in immediate termination of your services without notice
  • May result in your account being permanently banned from our services
  • May incur additional administrative fees that will be billed to your account

6. Service Termination by Keane Dev

If we terminate your service due to violations of our Terms of Service or Acceptable Use Policy:

  • No refunds will be issued for any remaining service period
  • We reserve the right to terminate services without prior notice for severe violations

7. Special Circumstances

In extremely rare and exceptional circumstances, management may review individual cases. However, please understand that exceptions to our no-refund policy are almost never granted and are entirely at our discretion.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Refund Policy, please contact our billing department at billing@keaneaudric.dev.

By purchasing our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this No-Refund Policy.
