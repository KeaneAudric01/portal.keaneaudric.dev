About Keane Dev

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Keane Dev emerged from a simple vision: to provide exceptional hosting solutions with unmatched reliability and performance. What began as a passion project by our founder, a dedicated system administrator with years of experience in the hosting industry, quickly evolved into a full-service hosting provider committed to excellence.

From our humble beginnings offering basic shared hosting services, we've expanded our portfolio to include a comprehensive range of hosting solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of our growing customer base.

Our Mission

At Keane Dev, our mission is to empower businesses and individuals with hosting solutions that combine cutting-edge technology, exceptional reliability, and outstanding customer support. We believe that hosting shouldn't be complicated, expensive, or unreliable, and we work tirelessly to ensure our services exceed expectations in every way.

Our Infrastructure

We've built our infrastructure with one goal in mind: performance. Our services run on premium AMD Ryzen processors, utilize high-speed NVMe storage, and operate on networks engineered for minimal latency and maximum uptime.

  • Data Centers: Strategically located in various regions to ensure optimal performance for clients worldwide
  • Hardware: Enterprise-grade components from trusted manufacturers
  • Network: Multiple tier-1 providers with automatic failover capabilities
  • Security: Advanced DDoS protection and regular security audits

Our Team

Behind every successful hosting company is a team of dedicated professionals. Our staff consists of experienced system administrators, network engineers, and customer support specialists who share a passion for technology and a commitment to service excellence.

Looking Forward

As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to our core principles: reliability, performance, and customer satisfaction. We're excited about upcoming service expansions, including VPS and dedicated server offerings, that will allow us to better serve our clients' evolving needs.

Thank you for considering Keane Dev for your hosting requirements. We look forward to the opportunity to exceed your expectations and be a trusted partner in your online success.
